Amazon has reduced its carbon intensity by 11.3% since 2019 and achieved 90% renewable energy across its global operations in 2023. The company diverted 2.7 million tons of packaging waste from landfills through improved recycling programs. Amazon committed to reaching net zero carbon emissions by 2040, ten years ahead of the Paris Agreement. The company pledged to deploy 100,000 electric delivery vehicles by 2030 and plans to invest $10 billion in climate solutions through the Climate Pledge Fund.
