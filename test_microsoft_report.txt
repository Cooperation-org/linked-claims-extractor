Microsoft committed to becoming carbon negative, water positive, zero waste, and protect more land than we use by 2030. Microsoft Cloud for Sustainability expanded to include Scopes 1, 2, and all 15 categories of Scope 3 carbon emissions tracking. The Planetary Computer provides open access to petabytes of environmental monitoring data. Microsoft Sustainability Manager helps organizations track progress across operations and value chains. The company faces new challenges from generative AI infrastructure and electricity requirements for meeting sustainability commitments.
