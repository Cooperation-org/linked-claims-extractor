Patagonia donated $100 million in climate profits to environmental organizations in 2022 and achieved carbon neutrality across its owned facilities in 2025. The company diverted 95% of waste from landfills and recycled 87 tons of used clothing through its Worn Wear program. Patagonia committed to become carbon neutral across its entire supply chain by 2030 and pledged to use 100% renewable or recycled materials by 2025. The company plans to regenerate organic cotton farming practices across 40,000 acres and aims to eliminate all virgin petroleum-based materials from its products by 2030.
